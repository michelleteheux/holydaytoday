Beyond the Garment

When we started The Simple Folk, our idea was simple: to create garments designed to last, helping reduce the need for constant replacement.

Over time, we realized it was about more than the clothes themselves. What matters just as much are the values woven into them: our commitment to responsible production, certified organic fabrics, fair partnerships, and thoughtful service. The Simple Folk is not only about what you wear but how its made and who it touches along the way.
